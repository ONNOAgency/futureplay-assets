Paste it into your email

Copy puts a ready-to-paste signature on your clipboard — not code.

« Gmail (web)

  1. Hit Copy for email on your signature above.
  2. Gmail → Settings (gear) → See all settings → General.
  3. Under Signature, create one, click in the box, paste (Ctrl/⌘+V).
  4. Set it as default for new mail & replies → Save changes.

« Outlook

  1. New / web: Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → paste into the signature box.
  2. Classic desktop: File → Options → Mail → Signatures → New → paste.
  3. Send yourself a test, then reply to it — confirm it holds.

Each person copies their own. You paste once and it's saved to your Gmail or Outlook account — it lives there, not on this page.
